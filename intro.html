Carbon Footprint Calculator

Your carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by our actions. Measuring your carbon footprint is the first step toward reducing it. The impact of carbon emissions is profound, affecting our climate, air quality, and health.

The impact of carbon emissions on the world includes:

  • Climate change and global warming
  • Sea-level rise
  • Extreme weather events
  • Loss of biodiversity
  • Health issues due to air pollution
